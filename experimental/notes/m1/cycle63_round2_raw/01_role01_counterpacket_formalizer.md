### 3.2 The block-collapse identity

This is the precise four-atom configuration relation behind the packet.

### 3.9 Exact effective group and periodic quotient

For

the additive group (F^3).

Fix (x_0\in H). Up to invertible rescaling of the additive coordinates, the
effective atom difference is
[

\longmapsto
\left(

\right).
\tag{3}
]

The multiplicative projections generate all of (H). The additive
projections span (F^3): otherwise there would be
((c_1,c_2,c_3)\ne0) for which
[

]
is constant on all (256) points of (H). Subtracting the constant would
give a nonzero polynomial of degree at most (3) with (256) roots.

Since (q\equiv1\pmod{256}), the subgroup generated by (3) contains the pure
multiplicative and pure additive factors. Consequently
[

\qquad
|G_{\rm eff}|=256q^3.

Choose a nontrivial additive character (\psi) of the prime field (F).
Characters of (G_{\rm eff}) may be indexed by
[

\mathbf Z/256\mathbf Z\times F^3.

[

\psi(c_1x+c_2x^2+c_3x^3).

A function is constant on cosets of some nontrivial subgroup of (H) only if
it is invariant under (x\mapsto-x), and invariance under ({\pm1})
is also sufficient. Now
[

=(-1)^a\psi(-2c_1x-2c_3x^3).

If (a) is odd, this cannot be (1), since the image of (\psi) consists
of (q)-th roots of unity and does not contain (-1) for odd (q).
If (a) is even, invariance forces
[
-2c_1x-2c_3x^3=0

for all (x\in H). A degree-three polynomial with (256) roots is zero, so
[

]

====================

holds. Therefore
[
\frac{N_{Q_{\rm per}}(\pi g)}{|\ker\pi_{\rm per}|}
<
\frac1{882}.

The periodic quotient therefore supplies less than one thousandth of a unit
of normalized charge to a full fiber containing more than

## 6. Exact failure point

The false implication is
[
\text{coherent exceptional mass}
\Longrightarrow
\text{periodicity of one-atom character pullbacks}.
]

For (K=\mu_4),
[
\prod_{u\in K}(X-cuZ)
=X^4-c^4Z^4
\equiv X^4\pmod{Z^4}.
]
The first three jet coordinates cancel only after four atoms are multiplied.
Most individual one-atom character pullbacks are nonperiodic, yet their
four-atom block products are perfectly coherent.

The fixed defect then destroys all support stabilizers without destroying
that four-atom coherence. This is exactly why (Q_{\rm per}) is formed at
the wrong level.
