# Terminal labels

- `STRATIFIED_COVER_CERTIFIED`: source bad-set completeness, branch partition,
  paid caps, chart caps, retained tags, same-field injections, AP/Gate-B
  obligations, uniform chart bound, field ledger, and numerator inequality all
  verify.
- `UNPAID_LOW_RESIDUAL`: the first uncovered or uncapped LOW source object.
- `UNPAID_BALANCED_COVER`: the first BALANCED chart/tag/injection/separator/cap
  failure after LOW passes.
- `UNPAID_HIGH_PLANE`: the first HIGH chart lacking a zero-dimensional cap
  mechanism; one separator on a plane is insufficient.
- `AP_DESCENT_FAILURE`: source AP_corr is absent/unverified, or is verified but
  Gate B fails without an official charge.
- `FIELD_LEDGER_MISMATCH`: any use or identification of fields contradicts the
  declared embeddings/orders, or a denominator other than q_line is used.
- `SOURCE_VALID_COUNTERPACKET`: an unbounded official family and the required
  reserve/charge/growth facts have been checked by a proof kernel.
- `MODEL_ONLY_STRESS_FAMILY`: finite/model evidence or unpromoted family data.
