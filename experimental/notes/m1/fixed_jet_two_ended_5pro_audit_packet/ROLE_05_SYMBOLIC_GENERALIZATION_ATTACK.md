# Role 05 - Symbolic Generalization Attack

Assume the transfer lemmas are true. Your job is to attack the dependence on
the 84-state product computation.

Use:

```text
fixed_jet_and_two_ended_transfer_note.md
experimental_to_papers_ad_recalibration_20260624.md
```

## Question

Can the computed product input

```text
#{Phi(T)} = 52,747,567,092
```

be replaced by a symbolic theorem or a parametric construction?

## Required Work

Analyze the fixed-jet and two-ended hypotheses abstractly. Try to produce one
of the following:

1. a parametric family of locator systems with common top jet and provably
   large distinct `P_J(beta)` spectrum;
2. a theorem explaining why the 84-state construction has large product
   spectrum without enumerating the spectrum;
3. a lower bound on distinct products from additive-combinatorial,
   character-sum, expansion, or algebraic-geometry methods;
4. a route cut showing that the present 84-state computation is probably
   isolated and should remain an example.

Do not merely say "use expansion" or "use character sums." Give an actual
lemma shape with variables, hypotheses, conclusion, and the first hard step.

If you see a route to a full proof family, state the next exact lemma.
