# Role 04 - Papers A-D Reusability Audit

Audit how the new transfer lemmas should fit into Papers A-D.

Use:

```text
experimental_to_papers_ad_recalibration_20260624.md
fixed_jet_and_two_ended_transfer_note.md
README.md
AGENTS.md
```

## Question

Which parts of the transfer note are reusable mathematics for the four-paper
program, and which parts are merely a computed example?

## Required Checks

- Does the fixed-jet lemma belong in Paper A, Paper B, or an appendix?
- Does the two-ended lemma strengthen Paper B's residue-line / support-wise
  MCA toolkit?
- Does the explicit `F_17^32` row add a new general theorem, or just a concrete
  negative example?
- Which objects should be archived away from the front-door proof narrative?
- What is the smallest theorem note that should be shown to a human
  mathematical referee?
- What should the next 3-4 cycles attack if the goal is "more proofs, fewer
  computations"?

Be blunt about bloat. If something is not reusable, say so.
