# ROLE 04 - Explicit Materializer

Your job is to produce the concrete data needed for a public replay artifact.

Do not merely say "generic y exists." Choose or specify a deterministic search
for a concrete `y`, and specify every object needed to replay the construction.

Required output:

1. Field model for `F0` and the separator extension.
2. Concrete candidate `y` or deterministic lexicographic search rule.
3. Domain construction for the preferred 464 package, or the fallback 560
   package if 464 fails.
4. Domain digest convention.
5. Formula for every support/locator in the two-copy construction.
6. Formula for final slope values.
7. Exact distinctness or multiplicity certificate.
8. Exact transversality certificate.
9. Exact frontier comparison against `floor(17^48/2^128)`.

If you cannot materialize the data, explain the exact blocker and give the
smallest checker that would remove it.
