# ROLE 06 - One-Line Syndrome / GRS Builder

Your job is to write the two-copy construction directly in syndrome-space or
GRS parity-check coordinates.

Required tasks:

1. Define the parity-check columns for the one-copy Role05 transfer.
2. Define the two-copy translated domain and its parity-check columns.
3. Produce the exact affine syndrome line `u + z v`.
4. Show that each selected support/complement span is hit by the line.
5. Identify the final slope coordinate in product/reciprocal-product form.
6. Prove transversality/noncontainment.
7. State exactly how projective separator multiplicity bounds same-slope
   collisions.
8. Verify all dimensions for either `(464,232,6,226)` or `(560,280,6,274)`.

If the construction cannot be written as one syndrome line, explain precisely
why and whether a different normalization fixes it.
