# Role 07 - AP-Visible Gate B Separator

Attack Gate B only after accepting the Cycle108 source-adapter requirements.

Prove or refute:

```text
L-CYCLE108-AP-VISIBLE-COMPLEMENT-LINE-SEPARATOR
```

Given an endpoint-corrected complement-line object satisfying the AP_corr
hypothesis supplied by Role 06, prove a polynomial active-theta bound with
exponent independent of `s` and `k`, or produce an AP_corr-valid complement-line
counterpacket.

Do not use a black-box finite separator unless you prove why AP_corr forces it
uniformly. If you give a checker, specify the exact terminal decisions and why
they imply the theorem without false negatives.
