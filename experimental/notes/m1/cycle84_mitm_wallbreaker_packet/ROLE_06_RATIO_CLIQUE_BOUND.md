# ROLE 06: RATIO CLIQUE BOUND

Your role is ratio-structure analyst.

If `m(v) >= 13`, then there are 13 compatible representations with common
product. Pairwise ratios between representations must satisfy strong seven-slot
constraints. Cycles 78-79 identified coherent common-ratio structure but did not
close it.

Try to prove:

```text
no coherent compatible ratio clique of size 13 exists.
```

or construct one.

Use:

- 4-slot product injectivity;
- min product distance at least 5;
- color compatibility;
- tau symmetry;
- left/right MITM split.

Deliverable:

- either a theorem excluding size-13 ratio cliques;
- or an explicit coherent ratio-clique counterpacket;
- or a smaller named finite wall.
