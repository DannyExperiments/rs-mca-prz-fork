# Role 03: Symmetric-Fiber Local-Limit Theorist

Attack the equivalent support/product formulation:

```text
For w = M|_D, bound the image size of
  S -> prod_{x in S}(beta-x)
on each elementary-symmetric prefix fiber
  e_j(S) = c_j, 1 <= j <= sigma-1.
```

Focus points:

1. Convert the bound to a statement about subset products on an
   elementary-symmetric prefix fiber.
2. Decide whether Macdonald/Newton identities, moment-curve geometry,
   Prouhet-Tarry-Escott structure, or finite-field local limit estimates can
   prove the required image bound.
3. Track repeated roots, collisions, and same-product degeneracy exactly.
4. If the target fails, identify the exact symmetric-fiber counterexample
   mechanism and whether it is source-valid.

Ambition bar: produce a proof of the support formulation or isolate the first
irreducible symmetric-product obstruction.
