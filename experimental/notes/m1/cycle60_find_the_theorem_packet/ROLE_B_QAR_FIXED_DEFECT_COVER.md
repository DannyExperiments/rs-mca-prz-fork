# ROLE B - QAR Fixed-Defect Cover Theorem

Find the cover theorem needed to make quotient charges finite.

Cycle 59 suggests individual fixed-defect quotient/action-rank packets are
understood, but one affine syndrome line might contain many such packets.

Prove or refute:

```text
W-MCA-QAR-FIXED-DEFECT-COVER
```

After tangent/common-envelope slopes are removed, all quotient-structured bad
slopes on one affine syndrome line are coverable by `n^{o(1)}` fixed-defect
quotient/action-rank packets.

If true, prove an explicit cover bound and show it implies the quotient term

```text
n^{o(1)} min{|F|, 2^{Q_H(eta)}}.
```

If false, construct one affine syndrome line with no envelope certificate and
superpolynomially many inequivalent defect packets, forcing a larger quotient
ledger.
