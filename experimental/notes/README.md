# Notes Index

Use these folders by research target:

- `audits/`: citation, theorem-label, import, and paper-consistency audits.
- `certificates/`: human-readable descriptions of JSON/script certificates.
- `domain/`: quotient-profile, shattering, circle/Chebyshev, and domain work.
- `f1/`: extension-line and residue-line MCA notes, including the Fable-loop
  review index in `f1/fable-loop/`.
- `l1/`: locator-fiber and generated-field list-local-limit notes.
- `l2/`: interleaved-list and dilation-constant notes.
- `m1/`: residue-line, support-packing, Kummer, and low-slack MCA notes.
- `m2/`: line-decoding bridge/separation notes.
- `protocol/`: ledger templates and protocol-facing material.
- `triage/`: PR triage records.
- `x1/`: list/CA/MCA conversion and separation notes.
