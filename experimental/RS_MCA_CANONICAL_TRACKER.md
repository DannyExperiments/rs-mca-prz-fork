This is the single canonical tracking document for the RS-MCA / Proximity Prize
work in this repository. It is a spreadsheet-equivalent route board: every row
has a stable item id, a claim level, evidence pointers, failed approaches, open
questions, and the next exact sub-goal.

The tracker is conservative. Raw model answers, Fable runs, and Pro returns are
inputs. They do not become theorem state until audited here or in a cited audit
file under literal labels such as `PROOF`, `COUNTERPACKET`, `BANKABLE_LEMMA`,
`ROUTE_CUT`, `EXACT_NEW_WALL`, or `AUDIT`.
